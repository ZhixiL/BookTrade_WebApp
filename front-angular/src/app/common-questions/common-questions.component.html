Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)



Why can't I post book/create buy order?

You can only do these things after you've registered and logged on.


Where can I find the details about a particular book?

Click book's name, it should take you to a detail page with all the information.


Where can find buy poster information?

Click on the post, then click the username on the top right corner.


Are descriptions required when posting a book?

No.


What's the difference between book-post and buy-order?

Book-post is for books that you want to sell, and buy-order is looking for potential seller for a particular book you want to buy
